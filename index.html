The Practices

Purpose: To embody responsibility, grief, and integration through a deliberate physical act.

  • Step 1: Naming the Old Form ("This form could not hold the truth")
  • Step 2: The Breaking (Recognition, not destruction)
  • Step 3: Gathering the Pieces ("Nothing is excluded")
  • Step 4: The Gold Bond (Honesty, Responsibility, Witnessed Truth)
  • Step 5: Integration ("I live as who I am becoming")

Purpose: To orient awareness beyond ego, steady the nervous system, and consciously transition into the Kintsugi Pottery Ritual.

Length: 12-15 minutes

  • Arriving & Settling the Mind

The result: A vessel more fragile in some ways, but stronger at the seams. Impossible to fake.
