The 12-Week Journey

Rupture → Stabilization → Reconstruction → Integration

Phase 1: Rupture & Survival (Weeks 1-2)
Awakening the Observer and letting go of the old story.

Phase 2: Stabilization (Weeks 3-4)
The Power of Now and walking the Middle Path.

Phase 3: Confrontation (Weeks 5-6)
The Law of Mirroring and managing energy and intention.

Phase 4: Reconstruction & Integration (Weeks 7-12)
Aligned action, the Soul's evolution, and the Golden Eternity.
